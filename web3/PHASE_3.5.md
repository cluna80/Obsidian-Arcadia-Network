## Contracts (4)

### 1. RiskProfiles.sol
Quantify risk for entities and worlds

**Risk Ratings:**
- Very Low
- Low
- Moderate
- High
- Very High
- Extreme

**Metrics:**
- Volatility (0-100)
- Stability Score (0-100)
- Failure Probability (0-100%)
- Historical Variance
- Risk Tolerance (Conservative/Moderate/Aggressive)

### 2. DerivativeMarkets.sol
Trade on future outcomes

**Derivative Types:**
- Call Options (bet price goes up)
- Put Options (bet price goes down)
- Futures (lock in future price)
- Swaps (exchange assets)

**Underlying Assets:**
- Entity Reputation
- Entity Performance
- World Success
- Token Price

### 3. InsuranceProtocol.sol
Protect against losses

**Insurance Types:**
- Entity Death Insurance
- Performance Guarantee
- Reputation Protection
- World Stability Insurance

**Example:**
```
Buy insurance for 1 ETH premium
Coverage: 100 ETH if entity dies
Duration: 90 days
```

### 4. OptionsExchange.sol
Options trading platform

**Features:**
- Write options (sell rights)
- Buy options
- Exercise options
- Automatic expiry

**Example:**
```
Write call option:
- Strike: 100 reputation
- Premium: 5 OAN
- Expiry: 30 days

If reputation > 100, buyer profits
```
